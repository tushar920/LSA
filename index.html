How It Works

A System, Not Sessions

A clear, proven process that ensures every student progresses — regardless of their starting point.

🔍

Assess

Baseline evaluation of fitness and skill levels.

02
💪

Structured sessions led by expert coaches.

03
📈

Continuous monitoring via app and reports.

04
🏆

Compete

Inter-school exposure and real match play.

Assess — Know Where Every Student Stands

Before training begins, we run a comprehensive baseline evaluation covering each student's fitness, coordination, and sport skill levels. This data powers everything — ensuring no student is left behind.

Train — Structured Sessions, Expert Coaches

Age-appropriate, curriculum-aligned 80-minute sessions led by certified coaches. Every session follows the LegaXy Training Blueprint — warm-up, skill focus, team activity, match play, cool-down.

Track — Data That Actually Means Something

We continuously measure progress against each student's own baseline. Schools get periodic reports, parents get visibility via the LegaXy app, and coaches use data to refine sessions.

Compete — Where Growth Becomes Real

Students get inter-school exposure and competitive opportunities — School Olympics, inter-house tournaments, and external circuits. Competing builds character and confidence.
